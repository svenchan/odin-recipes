Easy Pasta

This is an easy recipe and takes less than 30 minutes to put together. It always tastes good. Serve it with extra cheese for the little ones and wih some chilly flakes for tjose who like a bit of a kick.

If you are used to buying your tomato sauce ready made this might seem as more work, but as you get used to making your own sauce, you'll never buy it again. You can add fresh herbs in summer and if you like a warmer umami flavor yopu can add soy sauce too.

Ingredients (3 people)

  • Penne 500g
  • Canned tomatoes (cut) 2 cans
  • Chicken 300g
  • Garlic 2 cloves
  • Onion 1
  • Carrot 1
  • Olive oil 1tbs

Steps

  • Chop the garlic.
  • Dice the onion.
  • Cut the chicken in easy bite size.
  • Add oil to the pan on medium heat.
  • Fry the garlic untill it starts to give of nice smell.
  • Add the onion and fry untill it starts to look glassy.
  • Add the chicken and turn up the heat.
  • Fry the chicken until the edges start to colour golden brown.
  • Add the tomatoes, rinse the cans with some water to loosen up the left over juice and add it to the pan
  • Simmer for 10 minutes, half an hour if you have the time.
  • In a diffrent pot add water with 2 grams of salt and put on high heat.
  • When the water boils add the pasta and floow cooking instruction on package.
  • Finaly, strain the cooked pasta and add it to the sauce. Simmer together for a minute.
  • Serve with cheese and chilli on the side.
